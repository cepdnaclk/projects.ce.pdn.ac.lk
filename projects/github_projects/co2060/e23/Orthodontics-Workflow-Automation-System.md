The Orthodontics Workflow Automation System (OWAS) is a web-based platform that replaces manual dental records with a secure digital hub. By centralizing patient history and images, it enables instant data retrieval and faster care. Its modular, scalable architecture ensures future-readiness for integration across the entire Dental Hospital.
